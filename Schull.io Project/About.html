ABOUT US

Schull.io promotes a non-trditional approach to education in technology by
efficiently courses and providing training to specific individuals that have
passion for technology. We at schull.io are proud to have built a pipeline for cloud
computing engineers in an ever-expanding technological industry.



OUR STORY



Schull.io is an ed-tech platform that specializes in developing and placing
skilled individuals in Cloud Engineering, DevOps, Cybersecurity, Agile, and
Integration Engineering to meet the global demand for highly qualified
professionals in these fields.
Our trainee engineers receive a monthly stipend to cover the cost of
learning with support devices given. (where necessary) in addition, our
engineers are provided with fundamental agile and soft skills training. Our
programs are taught using a well-structured and internationally recognized curriculum.

OUR GOAL AND MISSION



Our mission is to build, train and deploy 10,000 world class technology
infrastructure engineers in Cloud engineers, DevOps, and cybersecurity in the
next five years. We help you kickstart your career and get you plugged in on
global opppurtunities regrdless of a background in tech. And our talent
pipeline includes training on soft skills required to thrive in the digital world.
To build a community of talented professionals based in Nigeria and Sub-
Saharan Africa, fit for migration and remote services in the emerging cloud and
virtualization technology.
